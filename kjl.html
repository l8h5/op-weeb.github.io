Complete Weed System

This is the most advanced weed system available for FiveM. It includes everything you need to create a realistic marijuana economy on your server.

Features include planting, growing, harvesting, processing, and selling with multiple levels of quality and effects.

Detailed Features

  • Planting System: Choose different strains with unique effects
  • Growing Mechanics: Watering, fertilizing, and plant care
  • Processing: Dry, cure, and package your product
  • Selling: Dynamic pricing based on quality and demand
  • Police Integration: Raid system and evidence collection

Installation Guide

  1. Download the script files after purchase
  2. Add the folder to your resources directory
  3. Add "ensure weed-system" to your server.cfg
  4. Configure the config.lua file to your needs
  5. Restart your server
